# Sales-Forecasting-Revenue-Trend-Analysis-Dashboard-
Interactive Power BI dashboard for sales forecasting, revenue trend analysis, YoY growth tracking, product performance evaluation, and regional revenue insights using historical sales data.

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing historical sales data and forecasting future revenue trends using Power BI. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue performance, growth trends, product contributions, and regional sales distribution to support business decision-making.

Business Objective

Organizations rely on forecasting to optimize inventory planning, marketing campaigns, budgeting, and production strategies. This project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can transform raw sales data into meaningful insights.

Dataset

The dataset contains:

- Monthly Sales Data
- Product Information
- Regional Sales Performance
- Revenue Metrics
- Forecasted Revenue Values
- Year-over-Year Growth Data

Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Forecast Revenue
- Average Monthly Revenue
- Average YoY Growth %
- Product-wise Revenue
- Region-wise Revenue Contribution

Dashboard Features

Revenue Analysis

- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue Forecasting Visualization
- Revenue vs Forecast Comparison

Product Performance

- Product-wise Revenue Performance
- Top Performing Products Analysis

Regional Analysis

- Region-wise Revenue Distribution
- Revenue Contribution by Region

Growth Analysis

- YoY Growth Monitoring
- Product-wise Growth Comparison

Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Forecasting Analytics

Business Insights

- Identified top-performing products and regions.
- Evaluated revenue growth patterns across time periods.
- Forecasted future sales trends for strategic planning.
- Highlighted areas requiring marketing and operational improvements.

Project Outcome

The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by providing a comprehensive view of sales performance, growth trends, and future revenue expectations.
